FASHION STORE

Clothing stores or shoe stores need to be able to use the matrix options for size and color.

Our System has what it takes for a small retail shop to be successful. Being a fully integrated package, it's easy to keep track of every transaction in your store from sales, stock movements, taxes and gross profit, right through to staff actions and even customer details.

Process Sales Fast

Ring up cash sales, lay-aways, invoices, quotes, take customer orders and identify unique sales opportunities quickly with the easy-to-use sales screen. New staff can be taught the basics within minutes.

Barcode Support

Our System will recognize manufacturers' bar code numbers or will create and print out customized bar codes.

Manage Stock

Create and maintain a detailed stock inventory, while simplifying the ordering, receiving and labeling of stock. Identify slower stock and get it moving and ensure your best sellers are always in stock.

Colour and Size Matrix

Clothing Stores have special needs, such that they enter a style or stock number such as JE1716 for a particular style. But the problem is the manufacturer supplies the JE1716 Jeans in different colours and sizes. Our System has a clothing matrix option that automatically allows the user to enter a matrix of all the colours and sizes that the store will carry.
The system will automatically track the JE716 item by creating sub items of itself. For example; JE1716-SIL-30 and JE1716-BON-30 are created from the matrix, and each item is tracked with it's own cost and selling price for sales and ordering.

Produce Reports At Point-Of-Sale

With just a couple of quick keystrokes and you can see exactly how your store is doing at any point in time–The system updates your stock inventory with each transaction, so you always know what's on lay-away, what's on order, and what your best-selling items are.
Check on the inventory report to know exactly which items are running low, which items on lay-by, which items on oder and what your best-selling items are.

Build Powerful Customer Relationships

With the ability to link transactions to customers, the system makes it easier to identify profitable customers and the additional sales opportunities they represent – improving your customer service and building stronger, more profitable customer relationships.

General Retail Pricing

This pricing system allows a standard retail price to be entered, but if more than a certain quantity are sold, it can be set up to automatically use a discount price(s). In addition, you can place items on sale between two given dates, and have a separate set of sale and discount prices.

Special Pricing

Some retail businesses will provide negotiated pricing to specific customers or corporations. The System can be set up to handle multiple price ranges for a specific item..

Printable Comments

You can attach a comment to each inventory item, and these comments will pop-up on the invoice so you are able to view or print it on the invoice itself.
